About

A personal record of my PhD research.

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Groningen. My doctoral research focuses on wild birds, ecological variation, and host-associated microbial communities.

This website is a simple space to record my PhD research, project development, academic notes, and related reflections.

Research

Urban pigeons

This project focuses on seasonal variation in urban feral pigeons in Groningen, with an interest in how diet, environment, and microbial communities may change across seasons and locations.

Skylarks

This project explores temporal variation in wild skylarks across months, years, and annual cycle stages.

Short-term changes in wild birds

This project examines short-term changes in wild birds and how these changes may relate to ecological and physiological conditions.
